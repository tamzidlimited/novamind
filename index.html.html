✦ FAQ

Questions?
We have answers.

Do I need to know how to code? ▼
Zero coding required. You paste a URL, the AI does everything — builds the funnel, writes emails, creates ads, trains the chatbot. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use NovaMind.
How does the AI know about my business? ▼
NovaMind's AI (powered by Claude) reads your website URL and extracts everything — your services, pricing, tone of voice, target customers, and USP. It then uses this to personalise every funnel, email, ad, and chatbot response.
Which channels can I connect? ▼
WhatsApp Business API, Instagram DMs, Facebook Messenger, Website Live Chat, and SMS via Twilio. All 5 channels feed into one unified inbox — the AI handles all of them simultaneously.
Can I take over from the AI mid-conversation? ▼
Absolutely. Every conversation has a "Take Over" button. When you click it, the AI steps back and shows you suggested draft replies instead. You can hand it back to the AI anytime. You're always in control.
Does it work with GoHighLevel (GHL)? ▼
Yes. NovaMind exports leads and pipelines directly to GHL via webhook. If you're a GHL agency, you can use NovaMind as your AI layer on top — or use our white-label Agency plan to offer it to your clients.
What happens after the 14-day free trial? ▼
You choose a plan. No auto-charge during the trial — you only pay when you decide to continue. Cancel anytime with one click. No contracts, no hidden fees.
